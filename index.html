Productivity Tips

How do I stay focused during work sessions?

Remove all distractions before starting. Put your phone in another room, close unnecessary browser tabs, and let others know you're in a focus session. Use noise-canceling headphones or white noise if needed.

What should I do during breaks?

Step away from your workspace. Stretch, walk around, do breathing exercises, or look out the window. Avoid checking social media or emails as this doesn't give your brain the rest it needs.

Can I customize the timer intervals?

Absolutely! While 25/5 is the classic Pomodoro ratio, you can adjust it to suit your needs. Some people prefer 45-minute work sessions with 15-minute breaks, while others work better with shorter 15-minute focused bursts.

How many sessions should I do per day?

Start with 4-6 sessions and adjust based on your energy levels. Quality matters more than quantity. It's better to have 4 highly focused sessions than 8 distracted ones.
